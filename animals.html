Animals!

Animal of the week!

Nana the orangutan

The name orangutan means "man of the forest" in the Malay language. In the lowland forests in which they reside, orangutans live solitary existences.

They feast on wild fruits like lychees, mangosteens, and figs, and slurp water from holes in trees.

Nana the orangutan is famed for his love of human interaction and loves it when people throw bannas into his enclosure :)
